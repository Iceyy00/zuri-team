The problem..

Young people face barriers in accessing quality tech education

Research has identified three significant barriers that young people face globally in accessing quality tech education...

Cost

Authentic high-quality tech education is often expensive, making it difficult for young individuals to afford...

Distance

Many young people are either engaged in jobs or school, limiting the distance they can travel to access tech learning opportunities...

Entrance Exams..

Tech schools, higher institutions, and boot camps often require pre-selection exams, which can be a significant barrier to entry...
